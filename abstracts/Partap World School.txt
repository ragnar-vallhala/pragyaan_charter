Future Skills and NEP Implementation

The National Education Policy (NEP) 2020 provides a transformative roadmap for preparing learners for an uncertain and dynamic future. It's a complete paradigm shift of our education system from rote learning to a skill based multidisciplinary system. NEP implementation moves towards heavy content based instruction to competency based and experiential learning. Being in a school set up I feel we need to emphasize more on skill development such as critical thinking, creativity, digital literacy, ethical reasoning, and adaptability.
