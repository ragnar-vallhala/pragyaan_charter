*Problem Statement*


Our education system is facing significant challenges in terms of following:


*Infrastructure* 

As we are living in a tech age, our education system lacks Uneven access to digital tools, modern labs, and quality facilities, particularly in rural and underserved areas.


*Equity Issues*


Inequality and imbalance based on gender, socio-cultural background, and geography have hindered universal access to quality education for most of the young generation.


*Skill Gaps*

 A predominant focus on rote learning has left nearly 80% of graduates without the critical thinking and technical skills required by modern system. These can't handle and compete with the modernised equipment, infra, technical tools and high tech operations present in every field.


*Adressal of the gaps*


An effective and efficient Methodology,

policies adoption and multi-pronged approach is required to address these gaps:


*Infrastructure* Establishing of latest labs, which can incorporate spirit of latest trends of technology among new generation in accordance with the National Educational policy.


*Equity:* 

Launching the Gender Inclusion Fund and providing targeted support for Socially and Economically Disadvantaged Groups (SEDGs).


*Skill Development* Mainstreaming vocational education and other skill development programs in the schools,with professionals, experts and trainees.
